# Available Tools

## Supabase
Full read/write access to all tables. Primary coordination mechanism with other bots.

### Key Tables:
- `bot_tasks` - Task assignments between bots
- `bot_activity_log` - Activity tracking
- `contacts` - Contact management

## Resend (Email)
Send emails from ava@kaldrtech.com
- Draft, send, and track emails
- Professional templates for different contexts

## Vapi (Voice/Calls)
Handle phone calls
- Inbound/outbound call management
- Voicemail and message taking

## Brave Search
Web search for research and lookups
- Answer questions requiring current information
- Find contact details, business info

## Telegram
Direct communication with LaSean
- Respond to messages and commands
- Send proactive updates and reminders
